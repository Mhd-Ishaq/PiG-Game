PIG GAME

Rules

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a,dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to hid ROUND score.
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost, After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to 'HOLD', which means that his ROUND score gets added to hid GLOBAl score. After that,it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 points on Global score wins the game
